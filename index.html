Questions

Everything you need to know

Is this really free?
Yes — completely free for you. We earn a commission from our energy partners when a switch completes. They pay us, not you. This is standard practice in the energy sector and does not affect your tariff price.
Is it safe to forward my bill email?
Yes. Your bill contains no sensitive payment data — just your consumption, CUPS code, and account number. We process it securely to extract the data needed for comparison and delete it after 30 days. We comply with GDPR.
I am renting — can I switch?
Yes, if the electricity bill is in your name. If your landlord pays the electricity, you would need to speak with them first.
How long until I see the savings?
The switch completes in 3–4 working days. Your first bill from the new provider typically arrives 30–40 days after the switch date, and that is when you will see the lower number.
Will my electricity be cut off?
Never. Switching suppliers in Spain only changes the billing company — the physical supply is never interrupted. Switch completes in 3–4 working days.
Which provider will I switch to?
We work with a growing network of licensed Spanish energy providers — currently Gana Energía and Nordy, with more being added. We compare tariffs across our partners against your actual usage to find the best match for your household.
What areas do you cover?
Currently active across Murcia, Alicante, and Costa Blanca. Expanding nationally. If you are elsewhere in Spain, send us your bill and we will let you know when your area is live.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your bill data is processed securely and used only to calculate your savings and submit your switch. We comply with GDPR and do not sell or share your personal data with third parties beyond our partner providers for the purpose of completing your contract.
