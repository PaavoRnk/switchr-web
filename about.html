Our story

Built by an expat, for expats.

Because switching energy shouldn't feel like filing taxes.

👤

Paavo Rannikmae

Founder, Switchr.es - Murcia, Spain

Product manager turned founder. 15 years building software that makes complicated processes simple - now applying that to Spanish electricity switching for English-speaking expats.

About a year ago I moved from Estonia to Murcia - mostly for the weather, if I'm honest. Estonia is a country where digital services just work. You renew your driving licence in two minutes, file taxes in five, and switch your electricity provider with a few clicks. It's unremarkable because it's expected.

Then I got my first Spanish electricity bill. It was higher than I expected, so I did what anyone would do - I started looking for a better deal. What followed was two hours of confusing comparison websites, phone calls in Spanish I wasn't fully comfortable with, and forms asking for codes I'd never heard of (CUPS, potencia, tramos... what?).

I didn't need someone to explain the Spanish energy market to me. I needed someone to just handle it.

I've spent 15 years in software - mostly as a product manager at various startups, working with engineers to build things that make complicated processes simple. So I built Switchr: an AI-powered service that reads your electricity bill, finds you a better tariff, and handles the entire switch. No forms, no phone calls, no power cuts. You upload your bill via WhatsApp or the website, and we take it from there.

The comparison is free and there's no lock-in. If we can't save you money, we'll tell you. And if we can, the switch typically takes a few days and you'll never notice it happened - except on your next bill.
