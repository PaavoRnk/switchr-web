How we calculate your saving — audit proof & tariff logic

All figures are calculated using actual metered consumption from the submitted bill, billing dates, and contracted power (potencia).

  • Annual totals are estimated using the supplier's stated yearly usage and today's published tariff rates
  • Energy prices include IEE (5.11%) and IVA (21%) as required by Spanish law
  • Potencia charges are based on the contracted 5.75 kW on both P1 and P2 — no power reduction is assumed
  • No change to electricity consumption habits is assumed — actual P1/P2/P3 split is used throughout
  • The net saving of €392 accounts for the slightly higher potencia rate on the new tariff (−€417 energy + €25 potencia)
