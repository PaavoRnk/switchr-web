How we calculated your saving — audit proof & tariff logic

All figures are calculated using your actual metered consumption from the bill you submitted, your billing dates, and your currently contracted power (potencia).

  • Annual totals are estimated using your supplier's stated yearly usage and today's published tariff rates
  • Energy prices include IEE (5.11%) and IVA (21%) as required by Spanish law

  • Optional services (Pack Iberdrola Hogar) are included in your current cost and excluded from the new cost, as they are not part of the new provider's offer
