Last updated: March 2026 Switchr · GDPR Compliant

1Who We Are

Switchr is an independent energy switching service operated by Paavo Rank (NIF/NIE: [YOUR NIE]), an autónomo registered in Spain.

We act as an authorised independent collaborator of Gana Energía, helping consumers and businesses in Spain find better electricity tariffs. We are not Gana Energía — we are a separate intermediary.

For privacy queries, contact us at: [YOUR EMAIL]

2What Data We Collect

When you use Switchr via WhatsApp, email, or website, we collect:

  • Identity: Full name, DNI/NIE number
  • Contact: WhatsApp phone number, email address
  • Supply: CUPS code, supply address, current provider and tariff
  • Financial: IBAN for direct debit (transmitted to your new provider)
  • Consumption: Annual kWh, contracted power, bill amount
  • Conversation: WhatsApp messages or emails during the switching process

Bill images or PDFs you send are processed by AI to extract the above data and are deleted immediately afterwards.

3Why We Process Your Data

  • Contract performance: To submit your switching contract to your new provider on your behalf
  • Legitimate interest: To calculate and display estimated savings
  • Legal obligation: To maintain records required under Spanish commercial law
  • Consent: To send WhatsApp or email notifications about your contract status

4Who We Share Data With

  • Gana Energía / Nordy — to process your switching contract. They become an independent data controller upon receipt.
  • Supabase Inc. — our database, hosted in the EU (Paris) under GDPR-compliant terms
  • Anthropic PBC — AI used to read your bill. Data is not retained for model training
  • Meta Platforms — WhatsApp Business API for message delivery
  • Google LLC — Google Analytics 4 (website analytics, only with your consent) and Google Fonts (font delivery via CDN)
  • Cloudflare Inc. — CDN delivery of third-party libraries (e.g. Chart.js)

We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

5Data Retention

  • Bill images: Deleted immediately after extraction
  • Contract data: 6 years (Spanish commercial law)
  • Conversation logs: 2 years, then deleted
  • IBAN and financial data: Deleted 30 days after contract activation

6Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to: access your data, rectify inaccuracies, erase your data, portability, object to processing, and withdraw consent at any time.

Contact us at [YOUR EMAIL] — we will respond within 30 days. You may also complain to the Spanish Data Protection Authority at aepd.es.

7AI Processing

Your bill image is processed by AI to extract data such as your CUPS code, power, and consumption. The image is not stored after extraction. Anthropic does not use your data to train AI models under our agreement.

In accordance with the EU AI Act, we disclose that AI is used for document processing in this service.

8Security

Data is stored on encrypted servers within the EU. We use HTTPS/TLS, access controls, and regular security reviews. IBAN and DNI data receive additional safeguards.

9Changes

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via WhatsApp or email. The current version is always at this URL.

10Cookies & Tracking

Essential storage

Switchr does not set any essential cookies. We use sessionStorage temporarily during the bill-upload flow (cleared when you close the tab) and localStorage for your cookie preference (sw_cookie_consent). Neither contains personal data.

Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. GA4 sets the following cookies:

  • _ga — distinguishes users (expires 2 years)
  • _gid — distinguishes users (expires 24 hours)
  • _gat — throttles request rate (expires 1 minute)

GA4 collects anonymised data including pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/region). IP addresses are anonymised before storage. We do not use GA4 for advertising. Google Analytics opt-out: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Third-party processors

  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log your IP address per their privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare CDN — used to deliver third-party libraries (e.g. Chart.js). Cloudflare may process request metadata.

Referral tracking

If you arrive via a referral link, we store a referral code in localStorage under the key sw_ref. This contains only a short alphanumeric code — no personal data. No expiry is set; you can clear it by clearing your browser's local storage.

How to change your preference

You can update your cookie preference at any time by clicking Cookie preferences in the footer of any page.

10Cookies y seguimiento

Almacenamiento esencial

Switchr no instala cookies esenciales. Usamos sessionStorage temporalmente durante el flujo de carga de factura (se borra al cerrar la pestaña) y localStorage para tu preferencia de cookies (sw_cookie_consent). Ninguno contiene datos personales.

Cookies analíticas (Google Analytics 4)

Con tu consentimiento, utilizamos Google Analytics 4 para entender cómo usan los visitantes nuestro sitio. GA4 instala las siguientes cookies:

  • _ga — distingue usuarios (caduca 2 años)
  • _gid — distingue usuarios (caduca 24 horas)
  • _gat — limita la tasa de solicitudes (caduca 1 minuto)

GA4 recoge datos anonimizados como páginas visitadas, tiempo en el sitio y ubicación general (país/región). Las direcciones IP se anonimiza antes de su almacenamiento. No utilizamos GA4 para publicidad. Exclusión de Google Analytics: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Procesadores de terceros

  • Google Fonts — las fuentes se cargan desde la CDN de Google. Google puede registrar tu IP según su política de privacidad.
  • Cloudflare CDN — se usa para entregar librerías de terceros (p. ej. Chart.js). Cloudflare puede procesar metadatos de solicitudes.

Seguimiento de referidos

Si llegas a través de un enlace de referido, guardamos un código de referido en localStorage bajo la clave sw_ref. Contiene únicamente un código alfanumérico corto, sin datos personales. No tiene fecha de caducidad; puedes eliminarlo borrando el almacenamiento local de tu navegador.

Cómo cambiar tu preferencia

Puedes actualizar tu preferencia de cookies en cualquier momento haciendo clic en Preferencias de cookies en el pie de página de cualquier página.
